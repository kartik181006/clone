Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers to common legal and business questions

Do I need an FSSAI license for selling homemade snacks online?

Yes, if you're selling food products online, you need an FSSAI license. For small-scale home businesses with turnover less than ₹12 lakhs, you can get a basic FSSAI registration. For higher turnover, you need a full FSSAI license.

What licenses do I need to open a beauty parlour in Delhi?

For a beauty parlour in Delhi, you need: 1) Trade License from MCD, 2) Shops & Establishments Registration, 3) Professional Tax Registration, 4) GST Registration (if turnover > ₹20 lakhs), 5) Health Department License for hygiene compliance.

How long does it take to resolve a civil case in India?

Civil cases typically take 2-5 years to resolve, depending on complexity and court workload. Commercial courts and fast-track courts may resolve cases faster. Alternative dispute resolution methods like mediation can resolve cases in 3-6 months.

What is the time limit for filing a civil suit?

Most civil suits have a 3-year limitation period from when the cause of action arose. However, this varies by case type: contract disputes (3 years), property disputes (12 years), recovery of money (3 years). Always consult a lawyer for specific cases.
